White Water Rafting

At White Water Rapids Co we believe every river trip should be thrilling, safe, and memorable. Our guides are certified, our equipment is maintained to the highest standards, and our mission is to connect people with wild water and the outdoors.

History

Founded in 2008 by a group of river guides and outdoor educators, White Water Rapids Co started as a small local outfitter and has grown into a regional leader in guided river adventures.

We blend local river knowledge with modern safety practices to deliver great trips for families, first-timers, and experienced paddlers alike.
